PlantTrees

The Plant Trees app is a mobile application designed to make tree planting simple, accessible, and engaging for everyone. The app's primary purpose is to promote environmental conservation by facilitating the planting and tracking of trees and creating meetups.

Development team: One developer and supervisor

How did I contribute?

Created the app's interface design and conducted target market research

Implemented front end features and backend database for user posts

Conducted tests to ensure all primary features were functioning

What was the result?

Approved by stakeholders and met its purpose

The app successfully completed its purpose and users were able to log trees planted and social with other users.

Received positive feedback from users

It received positive feedback for its user-friendly design and impactful mission.

Featured in university magazine

The project won an award and was featured in the university magazine.
